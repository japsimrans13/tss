About Us

The Technical Students Society (TSS) is a student body that is governed completely by the young blood students of the CS Department, GNDU (Amritsar). The Technical Students Society (TSS), an organization of the students, for the students and by the students, was established in the year 2003 as an answer to the student’s dire need for a larger platform where they could express new and old ideas, learn new skills, showcase their talents, get creative with their thoughts, interact, practice and grow.


After dissolving in 2006, the TSS, like a phoenix, was reborn in March 2008 from the ashes of its past. Since then it has successfully organized various creative, cultural, technical, and literary events and there has been no looking back.


The TSS is a legal society that has been registered as an NGO. Since its rebirth, it has been working to help students go beyond their defined limits and boundaries to discover new vistas and through its Light Bulb seminars, explore the amazing worlds of Animation, Internships, Hacking, and Open Source, Technical Languages, Interviews et. Al. To feed the food action deprived souls of the combatants we had the Apocalypse-our Gaming event. the welfare of the students of the department is the main priority we believe in leaving no stone unturned. From mock tests, to collage making, to tutorials and then creating part-time job opportunities, we’ve done it all.


We basically cater to the technical and non-technical interests of the students of the department. So the bottom line is- if you have an idea, execute it and if you need help and support bring it to us. We’ll see what we can do about it.
